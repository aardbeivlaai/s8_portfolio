FAQ

When do I need to apply for a Visa?

When coming to the Netherlands your visa application needs to be completed in order for you to start your first work day at ICT. The application process approximately takes four to eight weeks.

Which Visa process will I go through?

The visa process you will go through depends on your situation. The Immigratie-en Naturalisatiedienst (IND) is the governmental institution that handles all visa applications. There are several visa types that could be applicable to you.

For more information, please read the Visa page.
